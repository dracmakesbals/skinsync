The Ultimate Guide to Choosing the Best Second Cleansers

Welcome to our discussion on second cleansers, the essential step in your double cleansing routine! Whether you're a seasoned skincare enthusiast or just getting started, finding the right second cleanser can make a big difference in your skincare routine. Below, we’ve highlighted some of the top-rated second cleansers based on user experiences. Share your thoughts or find your next favorite cleanser here!

Top Second Cleansers

1. Simple Kind To Skin Moisturizing Facewash

  • Review Type: Rave
  • Price (range): ₹244-325 for 150ml
  • Availability in India: Online (Nykaa, Flipkart) and in physical stores
  • Skin Info: Oily, Acne-Prone
  • Ingredient List: Aqua, Sodium Laureth Sulfate, Decyl Glucoside, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Glycerin, PEG-55 Propylene Glycol Oleate, Sodium Chloride, Panthenol, Tocopheryl Acetate, Bisabolol, Anthemis Nobilis Flower Oil, Pelargonium Graveolens Flower Oil, among others.
  • Special Info: Soap-free, dye-free, fragrance-free, parabens-free, non-comedogenic, hypoallergenic.
  • Experience & Opinion: This facewash is praised for its effectiveness in cleansing without harshness. It’s particularly suitable for sensitive skin, providing a thorough clean while being gentle and moisturizing. Users appreciate its simplicity and effectiveness.
  • Repurchase Status: Holy Grail, Will Re-purchase

2. Rejuglow Face Wash

  • Review Type: Rave
  • Price (range): ₹275-300
  • Availability in India: Most pharmacies and online
  • Skin Info: Dry and Oily Skin
  • Ingredient List: 2% Glycolic, 1% Salicylic, 5% Aloe Vera, 0.5% Vitamin E. Full ingredient list not available.
  • Special Info: Dermatologist-prescribed for mild acne and post-acne hyperpigmentation.
  • Experience & Opinion: Known for its gentle exfoliation, this cleanser helps with post-acne hyperpigmentation over a few months of use. It’s a gentle option for various skin types.
  • Repurchase Status: Will Re-purchase, Holy Grail

3. Cetaphil Gentle Cleanser

  • Review Type: Rave
  • Price (range): ₹255 for 125ml
  • Availability in India: Widely available online and in select stores
  • Skin Info: Sensitive, Acne-Prone, Oily, Combination
  • Ingredient List: Aqua, Cetyl Alcohol, Propylene Glycol, Sodium Lauryl Sulphate, Stearyl Alcohol, Polysorbate 20, Methyl Paraben, Propyl Paraben, Butyl Paraben.
  • Special Info: Contains Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, recommended for damaged skin barriers and post-procedure use.
  • Experience & Opinion: A go-to for those with sensitive skin, it’s gentle yet effective. Ideal for post-peel or sensitive conditions but may need more product for thorough makeup removal.
  • Repurchase Status: Forever Repurchase

4. AHAGLOW

  • Review Type: Rave
  • Price (range): ₹575 for 200ml
  • Availability in India: Chemists and online
  • Skin Info: Oily and Acne-Prone
  • Ingredient List: Purified Water, Acrylate Copolymer, Sodium Laureth Sulfate, Aloe Vera, Polysorbate 80, Sodium Cocoyl, Apple Amino Acids, Decyl Glucoside, Glycolic Acid, Vitamin E, among others.
  • Special Info: Prescribed for acne treatment and effective with tretinoin.
  • Experience & Opinion: This cleanser excels in gently exfoliating and clearing whiteheads, managing sebum production, and aiding in the removal of dry skin flakes. The only downside is the bottle design.
  • Repurchase Status: Will Re-purchase
